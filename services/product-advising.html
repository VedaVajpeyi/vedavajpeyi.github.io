Product Strategy

Product Strategy Advising

For founders and leaders when the obvious explanations aren't adding up.

Work With Me

Who This Is For

Founders and product leaders facing decisions where the conventional wisdom doesn't quite fit, or where you need someone outside your day-to-day to pressure-test your thinking. High stakes. Ambiguous situation. You have data, but it's not clarifying anything.

What I Bring

I'm not here to validate your current direction or hand you a playbook. I'm here to help you see the real constraints in your problem, and then move fast with conviction toward the answer that actually serves your users.

That means I'll ask you questions that make you uncomfortable. I'll challenge inherited assumptions. I'll push you to be more rigorous about what you're actually measuring, and what success actually means in your context.

How It Works

Most engagements begin with a single focused conversation — 60 to 90 minutes on the specific problem you're navigating. We start with clarity on the core issue: not the surface problem, but the thing underneath that's actually blocking progress. What's the data telling you? What are you afraid of? Which trade-offs are explicit and which are being avoided?

Some clients come back for ongoing work — a monthly session to pressure-test direction, work through a critical hire, or think through a pricing or roadmap decision. Others need one session to get unstuck. Either way: you leave knowing what the right call is, and why you're making it.
