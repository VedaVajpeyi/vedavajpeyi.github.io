College Admissions

College Admissions Advising

For students aiming for selective universities.

Work With Me

Who This Is For

High-performing students aiming for top-tier universities (Ivy League, Stanford, MIT, Duke, Northwestern, etc.) who understand that grades and test scores are necessary but not sufficient. You need a story, strategy, and positioning that makes admissions committees understand who you actually are and why you belong in their community.

What I Bring

I've worked with admissions at top programs, and I understand what distinguishes a strong application from an unforgettable one. It's not about perfection. It's about coherence — a narrative that connects your background, your interests, your values, and your ambitions in a way that feels true and strategic.

I also bring the perspective of someone who's worked extensively on learning, community-building, and the sociology of how people grow. Admissions committees are looking for students who will contribute to their communities, and my work is helping you understand and articulate what that contribution looks like.

How It Works

We start with strategy: which schools align with what you're actually looking for? What's your authentic story? Then we work on positioning — finding the narrative that makes your application coherent and compelling.

Essay by essay, we make sure you're not just answering the question, but using each response to deepen the case for why you belong in that program. We focus on the things only you can say, the authentic vulnerability that makes admissions committees remember your application.
