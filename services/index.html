Work With Me

I trained as a sociologist and work in product strategy.

The gap between how organizations think people will behave and how they actually do is where I'm most useful.

I work with a small number of projects each year, usually when the data is inconclusive, the team is pulling in different directions, or the path forward looks sensible on paper but is not working in practice.

Product Strategy Advising

For founders and product leaders when the roadmap feels right but isn't working, the team is reading the problem differently, or the next move is still unclear. I help diagnose what is actually happening, not just optimize what is already there.

→

Career Coaching

For product leaders at an inflection point: a bigger role, a change in company, or a transition that is not moving as quickly as it should. We work on what your track record signals, how you are being read, and what needs to shift.
