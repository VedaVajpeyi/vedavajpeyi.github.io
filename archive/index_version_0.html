About

I'm a product leader and strategist who spent a decade building at scale — most recently as a Senior PM at Amazon, working on Alexa across voice AI, subscription, and hardware adoption. Before that, I founded No Desk Project, a platform for location-independent professionals that grew into a global community across six countries.

I approach product from a sociologist's instinct: I'm drawn to the gap between what metrics say and what humans actually experience. My best work has come from finding that gap and building into it — whether that was reaching 50 million users in 13 days for India's contact-tracing app, or turning a regulatory crisis into a $2M opportunity.

I hold an MBA from the University of Chicago Booth School of Business and was selected to attend the World Economic Forum in Davos as part of a delegation of global young leaders. I'm currently building Books & Beds in Bangalore — a literary café and bed & breakfast for curious travelers.
