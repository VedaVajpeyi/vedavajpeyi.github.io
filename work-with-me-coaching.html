WORK WITH ME

Career Coaching

For people ready to be read at the level they're actually operating at.

The Problem This Addresses

Whether you're just starting out, looking to level up within product, trying to break into big tech, or pivoting from another field: if there's a gap between how you work and how you're being read, that's what this work addresses.

The underlying problem is almost always the same. The rules you're being evaluated by were never made explicit. That gap shows up as near-miss interviews, offers at the wrong level, or the persistent sense that something isn't landing without being able to name what or why.

My work is about making that invisible logic visible.

The Lens This Work Is Built On

My sociology background, combined with Amazon and Chicago Booth experience, gives me a lens most career coaches don't have. Few people work across all three:

  • The social science of evaluation: how institutions actually assess judgment, potential, and readiness under uncertainty
  • The operational reality of big tech: how hiring loops, promotion panels, and senior leadership decisions function in practice at companies like Amazon, Google, and Meta
  • Analytical rigor: the strategic framing and decision discipline of a top MBA program

What that produces is the ability to read the social logic of evaluation systems. What signals committees are actually responding to, and why the same experience lands differently depending on how it's framed.

What We Work On

A focused set of working sessions built around your specific situation, not a generic curriculum.

  • Narrative Positioning — How you frame your story determines what level you signal. We move from a record of what you've done to a social signal of judgment and decision quality.
  • Resume & LinkedIn — Both documents need to reflect the level you're targeting, not the level you're at. We rewrite both with hiring managers and recruiters in mind.
  • Interview Preparation — The goal is understanding the evaluation logic underneath the question, not just rehearsing answers.
  • Job Search Strategy — A focused, high-signal approach covering where to look, how to prioritize, and how to use your network without it feeling transactional.
  • Salary & Offer Negotiation — Most people leave money on the table because they negotiate from the wrong frame. We cover both mechanics and psychology.

How We'd Work Together

An initial conversation to understand where you are, what you're targeting, and what's getting in the way. Then a focused set of working sessions built around your specific situation.

You leave with clear, reusable artifacts: a repositioned resume and LinkedIn aligned to the level you're targeting, interview storylines that hold up under pressure, a negotiation strategy if you're at the offer stage, and decision frameworks you can return to long after we're done.

Engagements are short and intensive by design. I work with a small number of people at a time, and each engagement gets full attention, not a slot in a roster.

This works best for people ready to do diagnostic work, when something real is at stake. If you're looking for accountability check-ins, broad career exploration, or someone to validate decisions you've already made, I'm probably not the right fit.
