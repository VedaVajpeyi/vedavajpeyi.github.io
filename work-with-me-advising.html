WORK WITH ME

Product Strategy Advising

For early-stage founders and 0→1 teams, the most expensive mistake isn't moving slowly — it's moving quickly in the wrong direction.

I provide light-touch, high-impact advisory for moments when the signals are noisy, the trade-offs are real, and internal alignment is harder than it should be. I'm most useful when the problem is genuinely messy and you need an outside perspective rooted in both Amazon-scale rigor and founder-level empathy.

Typical Engagements

  • Positioning Audits — Identifying whether you have a product problem — or a how-you-talk-about-the-product problem — before it calcifies.
  • GTM & Validation — Designing low-cost, high-signal experiments to validate product bets before over-investing in engineering.
  • Trade-Off Clarity — Facilitating the hard conversations teams often avoid, making decisions explicit, defensible, and aligned.
  • Sociological Friction Analysis — Surfacing the user tensions your metrics are hinting at but not fully explaining.

The Structure

I don't offer long consulting retainers. My advisory is built for speed and leverage.

  • Strategic Sprints — Targeted deep dives on a specific friction point.
  • Monthly Advisory — A standing strategic sounding board for founders navigating 0→1.
  • Gap Analyses — Focused assessments to clarify what actually needs attention next.

The goal isn't elegance for its own sake. It's clarity that holds up when things get messy.

How We'd Work Together

A scoping conversation to define the real problem, not just the visible symptoms. A short, focused engagement designed around decision-making — not deliverables. Clear next-step recommendations grounded in both user behavior and business reality.

I work best with teams who are open to being challenged and willing to make trade-offs explicit.
