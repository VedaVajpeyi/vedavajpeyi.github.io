WORK WITH ME

Speaking

On product strategy, the sociology of user behavior, and building for trust.

Past engagements include the World Economic Forum Annual Meeting in Davos (2020), the Google Launchpad Pioneer Summit at Google HQ, and academic settings including Chicago Booth's LEAD program.

I speak to audiences of practitioners, executives, and students on topics at the intersection of product thinking, behavioral sociology, and organizational clarity.

Topics

  • Product Strategy Through a Sociological Lens — Why the hardest product problems are human problems in disguise, and how to see them.
  • The Sociology of User Behavior — How people actually make decisions under constraint, and what that means for product teams.
  • Building for Trust — The asymmetry of trust in digital products, voice interfaces, and high-stakes deployments.
  • Metrics, Measurement, and the Problems They Hide — A framework for auditing inherited assumptions before they become strategy.
