About

I'm a product leader and entrepreneur based in Brooklyn, NY. At Amazon, I work on Alexa — building voice experiences that reach millions of people. Outside of that, I'm focused on the future of remote work and what it means to build a meaningful career without being tied to any single place.

I founded No Desk Project because I believed the shift to remote work was more than logistical — it was a cultural transformation. NDP brings together location-independent professionals for immersive, curated experiences around the world: work that travels with you, and a community that makes it worthwhile.

I also lead Nomadize, a platform helping digital nomads find community, workspace, and infrastructure wherever they choose to be.

I hold an MBA from the University of Chicago Booth School of Business, where I also discovered some of my most enduring community.
