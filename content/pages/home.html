About

I am a product leader and strategist who studied people long before I ever studied product. My background is in sociology — that's the foundation of how I work.

At Amazon, I work on Alexa. I've founded companies for the remote generation. And right now, I'm building a boutique hotel in Bangalore called Books & Beds.

With an MBA from Chicago Booth and a heart for sociology from UCLA, I build with both rigor and empathy — not for a "persona" in a slide deck, but for the whole, specific, complicated person on the other end.
