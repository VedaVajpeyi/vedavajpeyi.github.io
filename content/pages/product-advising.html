Product Strategy Advising

Who This Is For

Founders and product leaders facing decisions where the conventional wisdom doesn't quite fit, or where you need someone outside your day-to-day to pressure-test your thinking. High stakes. Ambiguous situation. You have data, but it's not clarifying anything.

What I Bring

I'm not here to validate your current direction or hand you a playbook. I'm here to help you see the real constraints in your problem, and then move fast with conviction toward the answer that actually serves your users.

That means I'll ask you questions that make you uncomfortable. I'll challenge inherited assumptions. I'll push you to be more rigorous about what you're actually measuring, and what success actually means in your context.

How It Works

We start with clarity on your core problem — not the surface issue, but the thing underneath that's actually blocking progress. Then we move through the decision systematically: what's the data telling you? What are you afraid of? Which trade-offs are explicit and which are being avoided?

The goal is never agreement. It's clarity. You leave knowing the right decision is, and why you're choosing it.
