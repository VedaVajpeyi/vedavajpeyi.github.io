Career Coaching

Who This Is For

Product leaders and operators who are at a transition point — moving to a bigger role, changing companies, taking on new responsibility — and want to approach that transition strategically instead of just falling into the next thing.

What I Bring

I've navigated multiple transitions: from startup to scaleup, from IC to leadership, from builder to advisor. I've also watched hundreds of people navigate these transitions, and I know what distinguishes the ones who step into their next role with impact from the ones who are still figuring out what their role actually is six months in.

My work is to help you think strategically about what you actually want, what leverage looks like in your next environment, and how to position yourself for impact.

How It Works

We start with honest assessment: what are your actual strengths? Where do you add the most value? What environments bring out the best in you? Then we work on positioning — for the role you want, for the team you want to join, for the kind of leader you want to become.

We'll work on narrative too: how do you tell your story in a way that's authentic and compelling? How do you make it clear why this next role makes sense for you, and why you're the right person for it?
